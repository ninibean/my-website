About Me

Who I Am

I am a computer science student attending North Carolina A&T State University. I am a programmer, a creative, a professional stress baker, and the oldest in a set of twins. I am the founder of Earthbound, a greentech startup company.

What I am Passionate About

I want to inspire people to step outside of the box and not conform to or be pressured by stereotypes. I am passionate about combining creativity and art with technology to solve the world's problems. I want to explore that intersection of art and technology throughout my career. Coding aside, I want to give back to my community as much as I can. Food insecuirty, homelessness, racial inequality, and climate change are problems that I am particualy interested in solving.

What's The Deal with the Space Background?

Fun fact: I love astronomy.
Seriously though, when I think of space, I think about the concept of infinity. Anything can happen in space. There are things out there that exist but aren't discovered yet. Paradoxes exist in space. I apply that logic to myself and the way I think. I try to look outside the box and solve problems in ways that people haven't thought about. I am methodical and efficent in the way I strategize and complete my tasks, but my final products always have a creative spin to them.
